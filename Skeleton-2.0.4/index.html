What are some of my favorite games? Well, I thoroughly enjoy games with a great story. Gameplay comes second, and then graphics. I have a belief that everytime you play a game, you try to learn a lesson from it. What is it trying to teach? What is the takeaway message? One of the best games I've ever played is Hotline Miami, 1 and 2. I could go indepth for hours about the story for both, but there's really no need for that, you can see those theories on Youtube. Basically, it taught me that humans are animalistic and will try to execute and purge their violence as soon as they have an excuse.

Other notable games that I've played include Far Cry 3, Max Payne 3, and Battlefield 3.
